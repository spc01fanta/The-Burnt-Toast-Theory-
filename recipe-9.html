Ingredients
  • 1 banana (preferably not moon-ripened)

  1. Begin your fruity adventure by gathering your ingredients. Locate the ripest banana in the bunch.
  2. Grab a handful of mixed berries from your fridge or freezer – strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, whatever tickles your taste buds.
  3. With all your ingredients assembled, it's time to blend, baby, blend! Fire up your blender and watch as your humble ingredients transform into a colourful whirlwind of fruity goodness.
  4. Once your smoothie reaches the perfect consistency – smooth, creamy, and oh-so-irresistible – pour it into your favourite glass.
  5. Now comes the best part – taste testing! Grab a straw, take a sip. Ah, the sweet taste of success
