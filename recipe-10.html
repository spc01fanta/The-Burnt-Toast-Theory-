Shell Shock Wonder: A Boiled Egg
Calories : A humble handful
Protein: Eggs-actly what you need to keep you going
Ingredients
  • Eggs (as many as your heart desires, or as many as your pot can handle)
  • Water
  • A pinch of salt and pepper

  1. Begin your egg-cellent adventure by carefully placing your eggs in a single layer at the bottom of a saucepan or pot.
  2. Pour cold water over the eggs until they're submerged by an inch or two.
  3. Place the pot on the stove and bring the water to a gentle boil over medium-high heat.
  4. Set a timer for your desired egg doneness. For soft-boiled eggs with a gooey yolk, aim for about 6-7 minutes. For firm-boiled eggs with a perfectly set yolk, go for 9-12 minutes.
  5. While the eggs work their magic, prepare a bowl of ice water. Once the timer goes off, promptly transfer the eggs to the ice bath using a slotted spoon.
  6. Let the eggs chill out in the ice water for a few minutes then once their cool peel away the shell, revealing the bouncy, beautiful egg inside.
  7. Voila! You've mastered the art of bouncy boiled eggs. Enjoy them solo, with a sprinkle of salt and pepper, or maybe even with the toast you learnt to make earlier if your feeling brave.
