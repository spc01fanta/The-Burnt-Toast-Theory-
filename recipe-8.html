A Warming Soothing Cup of Bilss: Tea

Calories : Practically non, perfect for that summer bod
Happiness Boost: Guaranteed

Ingredients

  • 1 tea bag
  • Water (preferably hot, but room temperature works in a pinch)
  • Optional extras: honey, lemon, a splash of milk (because tea is all about personalization)

  1. Begin your tea adventure by selecting the perfect teabag from your stash. Will it be classic English Breakfast or maybe a daring Chai ?
  2. Boil some water in your trusty kettle, or if you're feeling particularly lazy, simply microwave a mug of water until it's hot enough to be a hazard
  3. Now, it's time to introduce your teabag to its hot water bath. Gently lower it into the mug, giving it a little swirl to ensure maximum flavour infusion. Feel free to whisper words of encouragement. A happy teabag makes for a happy cuppa.
  4. While your tea steeps, ponder life's greatest questions: ‘What is the meaning of existence?’ For example.
  5. Now comes the fun part – customization! Add a dollop of honey for sweetness, a squeeze of lemon for zest, or a splash of milk for creamy goodness.
  6. Finally, cradle your mug in your hands, take a sip, and let the warm embrace of tea envelop your soul.
