Vitamins and Minerals: All toasted out for the extra crunch !
Satisfaction: Guaranteed or your money back (we dont have money)
Ingredients
  • 2 slices of mold free bread
  • Butter, Jam, or your spread of choice

  1. Begin by rummaging through your bread stash in search of two slices that haven't achieved rock-like consistency. If all else fails, resort to using a chisel.
  2. Once you've found your bread (congratulations!), pop those slices into the nearest toaster. Adjust the settings according to your preferred level of toastiness – from lightly golden to "charcoal chic."
  3. While your bread undergoes its miraculous transformation, locate your spread of choice.
  4. Once your toast emerges from the toaster, handle it with the utmost care. Hot toast is like a delicate butterfly – beautiful, but potentially dangerous.
  5. Now it's time to get creative with toppings. Whether you're in the mood for classic butter and jam, or feeling adventurous, the sky’s the limit
  6. Finally, grab a plate, a napkin (or not), and take a moment to admire your handiwork. You've successfully transformed humble bread into a culinary masterpiece
