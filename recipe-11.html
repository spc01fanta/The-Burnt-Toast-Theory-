  1. Start by wrestling that can open like it's the final round of a cooking show showdown
  2. Transfer the contents of the can into a microwave-safe bowl or saucepan if you're feeling extra gourmet !
  3. If it looks like sludge, add a splash of water or broth to thin it out. If it looks like water, you've gone too far.
  4. Heat your soup on the stove or in the microwave until it's nice and steamy.
  5. stir then season to taste. Got some questionable spices lurking in the back of your pantry? Perfect!
  6. Serve it up in your fanciest bowl then dig in with reckless abandon
